Mole

Mole is a spicy and savory dish derived from Oaxaca, Mexico. While there are various styles found around mexico, most have the same base. It is made by roasting dried spices and chili peppers. The ingredients are combined and pureed into a thick sauce. The sause is served with any protein with the most common option being chicken.

Ingredients
  • 3-4 pounds of chicken
  • 5 whole peppercorns
  • 5 whole cloves
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1/2 teaspoon anise seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon coriander seeds
  • 6 dried guajillo chili peppers
  • 4 dried chile ancho peppers
  • salt
  • 1/4 cup of almonds
  • 1/4 cup of pumpkin seeds

Directions

  1. Add chicken and water to pot. Simmer for 40min
