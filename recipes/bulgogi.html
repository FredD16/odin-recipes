Bulgogi

Bulgogi is a korean dish consisting of marinated thin slices of meat, served with rice and cooked egg.

Ingredients

  • 5 tbs soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup Chopped Green Onion
  • 2 1/2 tbs White Sugar
  • 2 tbs Minced Garlic
  • 2 tbs Sesame Seeds
  • 2 tbs Sesame Oil
  • 1/2 tsp Ground Black Pepper
  • 1 lb Flank Steak(thin)

Directions

  1. In a bowl, mix meat, soy sauce, green onion, garlic, sugar, Sesame oil, and pepper
  2. Let meat marinate for 4-24 hrs
